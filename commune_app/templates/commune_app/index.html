Our Story

The Communet platform allows people with shared interests and values to create a financially cooperative community, with pre-established norms and procedures. Chores, shopping, and bills are all pre-configured and agreed upon before co-habitation begins, taking the mess and complexity out of starting a shared life. Future chores, expenses, and savings can be added and voted on at any time.
